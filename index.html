Welcome to the House of Mysteries...

The House of Mysteries is a bookstore website dedicated to detective fiction and mystery stories.
Whether you're a fan of Arthur Conan Doyle, Agatha Christie or Stephen King, the House of Mysteries has the book for you!

Sherlock Holmes


One of the greatest fictional detectives of all time, Sir Arthur Conan Doyle's iconic character has encountered no shortage of difficult cases. With his genius mind and loyal assistant Doctor Watson at his side, however, few can get away with murder!


Hercule Poirot


Agatha Christie's Belgian detective is known as much for his eccentricities as he is for his intellect and case-solving methods. He claims to value psychological evidence more than physical - but that doesn't change the fact that he's cleared up many a daunting case!

From Page to Screen


"Murder on the Orient Express" is one of Agatha Christie's most iconic mysteries - so, unsurprisingly, it was the first picked to get a film adaptation. With a colorful cast, including Keith Branagh - who both plays detective Hercule Poirot and directs the move - Murder on the Orient Express is sure to keep you hooked, even on the silver screen instead of on paper.
